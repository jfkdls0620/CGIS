Is GHG bad?What’s GHG?

GHG is any gaseous compound surrounding the earth that helps maintain its appropriate surface temperature.
However, these days it is considered a major factor in global warming because of the excessive emissions.

GHG maintains the appropriate surface temperature of the earth.

Too much GHG makes the earth sick.

The Earth crisis story

Too much GHG makes the earth sick.
How can we recognize this crisis of the earth?

When GHG increases excessively, climate change occurs.
