Inventory Guideline

Guideline for building up
GHG inventory for the province

Background
and purpose
The setting-up of the inventory is based upon the guidelines for Korean local government
greenhouse gas inventories (K-eco(2017)-RF10-21) and IPCC guidelines (IPCC G/L 2006),
proposed according to the condition of developing countries. In this way, the inventory
can be set-up on consideration of the actual situation in Cagayan Valley.
Key aspects
of the guideline
The guideline consists of part 1-introduction and part 2- GHG inventory quantification.
PART I. INTRODUCTION
General content including inventory boundary, Activity data,
emissions quantification, quality and data uncertainty
verification method and so on.
PART II CACULATING EMISSIONS
Introduction about emissions quantification method
by sector-Energy(3), IPPU(7), AFOLU(9), Waste(4),
Indirect Emission(3)
